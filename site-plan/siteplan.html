Rehoboth’s Hub

Site Name

Rehoboth’s Hub is a boutique which is not doing badly offline, selling assorted and exclusive unisex wears and aparels for any class or taste of a person.

Optional domain availability: rehobothshub.com

Site Purpose

This project is chosen to meet the need of a local boutique which is not doing badly offline, but have a chance to do even better with a good online platform. On the website, visitors will be able the view available products, products specification and place an order by filling out a form. Payment gateways will not be part of the scope for now.

Scenarios

  • What is this Boutique all about?
  • Will they have what I need?
  • How will I be able to make purchase?
  • How will my items be delivered to me?

Color Schema

  • Sea Green (#2E8B57): Used for headings, navigation, and borders.
  • Orange (#FFA500): Used for call-to-action buttons, icons, and accents.

Typography

  • Roboto (Sans-serif): Used for body text for readability.
  • Merriweather (Serif): Used for headings to create a classic academic feel.
